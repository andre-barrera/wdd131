La Bella Gelateria

Website Planning Document

Site Name

La Bella Gelateria - This name reflects an elegant brand and promotes real Italian Gelato.

Reason for choosing this subject: I chose this topic because a family member currently has a successful small gelato shop but doesn't have a webpage. Many small businesses miss opportunities to increase sales through an online presence. This site would help promote the shop and connect with more customers.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to promote and provide only presence for this gelato shop. It is planned to show show a menu of different gelato flavors, also to show store information, address and contact details.

Scenarios

  • What are the most popular gelato flavors available right now?
  • What gelato presentations are available?
